Moncler

Assistant Producer – E-commerce and Digital Studio

As part of Moncler's Digital Studio, I supported the coordination of digital productions and e-commerce campaigns, contributing to the creation of consistent and high-quality visual content. The role combined organization, technical oversight, and a sensitivity to the brand's visual identity.

Digital Production

I assisted in managing daily production workflows - from product campaigns to lookbooks - helping to align creative direction with operational needs. This included coordinating timelines, ensuring assets were delivered correctly, and maintaining accuracy across digital platforms.

Product Focus

A key part of my role involved supporting the product preparation and selection process. I worked closely with the merchandising team to ensure priorities and brand guidelines were respected, coordinated logistics to guarantee product availability on set, and assisted the styling team in highlighting key items with strong commercial relevance.

Collaboration

Working alongside the internal creative team and external collaborators, including stylists Karl Templer and Imruh Asha, I supported productions where design precision and brand storytelling came together. These collaborations offered insight into the detailed process behind large-scale creative projects.

On Set & Coordination

On set, I provided logistical and organizational support - liaising with agents, vendors, and technical teams to ensure smooth execution. My contribution focused on keeping communication clear, schedules on track, and production details under control.

E-commerce Workflow

I also helped streamline the e-commerce content pipeline by monitoring product flow, updating inventories, and supporting the integration of new assets across online channels. The goal was to ensure consistency between the creative output and the customer-facing digital experience.

Key Projects

  • Lookbook FW24
  • Product Campaign FW24
  • Re/Icon Campaign
  • Product Campaign SS25

Impact

Through this experience, I gained a comprehensive view of digital content production within a global luxury brand - understanding how creativity, structure, and product awareness work together to define a visual identity that feels both consistent and elevated.
