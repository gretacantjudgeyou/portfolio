Moncler

Working within Moncler’s Digital Studio, I supported the creation of campaigns and special projects that defined the brand’s online presence. From technical shootings to large-scale digital productions, I coordinated teams, vendors, and logistics—ensuring that every launch carried the same precision and impact as the brand itself.

Digital Craft

At the heart of my role was the orchestration of Moncler’s digital production process. From campaign photography to product launches, I helped align creative and operational teams to deliver high-quality content on schedule. Every asset—whether a product shot or a lookbook—was treated with the same meticulous attention that defines the brand.

Collaboration

Working alongside visionary stylists such as Karl Templer and Imruh Asha, I supported productions that blended strong creative direction with flawless execution. These collaborations were an opportunity to see Moncler’s aesthetic come to life through different lenses, reinforcing the brand’s reputation for both innovation and style.

On Set & Beyond

My responsibilities extended from managing on-set productions to handling budgets and coordinating with agents and vendors. Whether it was a high-pressure campaign shoot or a technical e-commerce session, I ensured smooth operations, clear communication, and efficient problem-solving.

Flow

Beyond campaigns, I optimized product flow for e-commerce: updating inventory, monitoring suppliers, and ensuring accuracy across platforms. It was about making the digital experience seamless—from back-end logistics to the final customer journey.

Connections

What stood out most was the intersection of creativity and precision: collaborating with top stylists, managing production details, and ensuring delivery across multiple platforms. It was an environment where high fashion met high functionality—and where every detail mattered.

Impact

These projects strengthened Moncler’s digital presence, ensuring that every campaign resonated online with the same strength it had on set. By balancing creativity with structure, we built a digital ecosystem where content, product, and storytelling worked hand in hand.
