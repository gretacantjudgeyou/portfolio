Capri Music Awards

Producer

For the inaugural edition of the Capri Music Awards, I collaborated with the three founding organizers - Magellano Concerti, Golden Boys S.R.L., and the Municipality of Capri - as an external producer supporting the overall coordination of the event. My work focused on connecting creative, operational, and logistical aspects to ensure the seamless realization of a complex multi-day production.

Vision

The goal was to create a cultural event capable of merging Capri's timeless setting with the energy of contemporary music. The challenge was not only aesthetic but structural: transforming a historic venue into a stage where lighting, sound, and emotion could coexist in harmony.

Production & Coordination

As part of the production team, I oversaw the preparation of production documents and support materials, managed logistics for both technical teams and artists, and coordinated with the tour manager to align all operational aspects. The event spanned three consecutive days, requiring precise scheduling, equipment management, and on-site supervision.

Logistics & Site Management

Organizing a large-scale live event in Capri posed significant logistical challenges - from the transport and setup of heavy equipment to the coordination of staff and suppliers across limited access points. Ensuring that all materials, instruments, and technical infrastructure reached the island on time demanded careful planning and continuous communication with Magellano Concerti and local authorities.

Artist & Talent Relations

Within the Capri Young Awards program, I acted as the main point of contact for emerging artists, facilitating communication, scheduling, and backstage coordination. Supporting new talents in a high-profile environment required empathy, clarity, and adaptability - helping to build a professional yet welcoming atmosphere.

Operational Strategy

Behind the scenes, precision and communication shaped the project's foundation. From managing supplier timelines to ensuring compliance with the constraints of a UNESCO heritage site, I helped maintain balance between artistic ambition and logistical reality. Each decision aimed to preserve both the integrity of the venue and the flow of the event.

A New Chapter for Capri

This project’s success stemmed from collaboration - between producers, artists, technical teams, and local institutions. The collective commitment of everyone involved turned an ambitious idea into an experience that resonated with both the island’s history and its creative future.

Impact

The first edition of the Capri Music Awards set a new precedent for live cultural events on the island. Managing three days of performances, logistics, and production in such a unique setting offered tangible proof of what can be achieved through organization, teamwork, and precision under pressure.
