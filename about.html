Frequently Asked Questions

1. What makes Greta different from other agencies?

We are a network of independent talents who collaborate on carefully chosen projects, focusing on innovation, sustainability, and authentic human connections rather than traditional agency structures.

2. Do you work with projects that are still pre-launch?

Yes, we specialize in helping early-stage projects and pre-launch companies develop their strategy, brand identity, and go-to-market approach.

3. How do you measure success and report back to clients?

We use clear, measurable KPIs and provide regular reports with actionable insights. Success is measured by both quantitative metrics and qualitative impact on your business goals.

4. How do you source and vet influencers for campaigns?

We have an extensive network of vetted influencers and KOLs across various industries. Our selection process focuses on authenticity, engagement quality, and alignment with your brand values and target audience.

5. How quickly can we kick off a campaign and what does onboarding look like?

We can typically start a campaign within 1-2 weeks after initial consultation. Our onboarding process includes strategy development, timeline planning, and team alignment to ensure smooth execution from day one.
